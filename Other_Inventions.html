Other Inventions

At 19 years old, Blaise Pascal wanted to help his father with math, and so he invented the pasculine (calculator?) to help out his dad.


Wikimedia Commons Image

Incidentally, Blaise Pascal invented the Hydraulic press during an experiment, with the press following the principle (pascal's law) that he'd come up with earlier in order to evenly apply force onto subjects.
